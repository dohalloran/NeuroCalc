About NeuroCalc

The Nernst equation has a physiological application when used to calculate the potential of an ion of charge z across a membrane. This potential is determined using the concentration of the ion both inside and outside the cell

The Goldman–Hodgkin–Katz voltage equation, more commonly known as the Goldman equation, is used in cell membrane physiology to determine the reversal potential across a cell's membrane, taking into account all of the ions that are permeant through that membrane

NeuroCalc was developed by ohalloranlab for Android and ios devices in 2018, and serves as a user friendly app that aims to help students calculate the Nernst reversal potential and Goldman Hodgkin Katz equation.
